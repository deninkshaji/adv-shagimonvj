Shagimon V J is a seasoned educator and legal professional with a unique dual-career background. He earned his LL.B. from Government Law College, Thiruvananthapuram, early in his career, before entering the education sector. He served for several decades as a Higher Secondary School Teacher and later as Principal, gaining deep insight into academic systems and administrative processes.
After retiring from government service, he transitioned to full-time legal practice. He is currently associated with P P Aboobacker Associates in Taliparamba. His legal practice focuses on educational service rules, staff-related disputes, and institutional legal consultancy. It also spans consumer rights, human rights, employment rights, and family law. Leveraging his combined experience in education and law, he provides expert legal counsel to individuals and institutions across Kerala.
